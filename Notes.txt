1. VideoMAE (Best choice)

Model: MCG-NJU/videomae-base or MCG-NJU/videomae-base-finetuned-kinetics
Input: 16 frames × 224×224 (can adjust)
Why: Handles cropped regions well, pre-trained on diverse actions, excellent fine-tuning performance
Use case: Extract 16-frame crops from tracked bounding boxes

2. TimeSformer

Model: facebook/timesformer-base-finetuned-k400
Input: 8-96 frames (flexible)
Why: Efficient temporal modeling, good for variable-length sequences

3. ViViT (Video Vision Transformer)

Model: google/vivit-b-16x2-kinetics400
Input: 32 frames × 224×224
Why: Pure ViT architecture for video
